About me

My name is Natnael Abay. I was born in 1999 Dec in a place called Gondar. I lived in Gondarfor only 2 until my grand mother my died, but then me and my family moved to Bahirdar that was not far from my birth place and I have been living there ever since.

My first exposure to a computer was at my 8th birth day when my father bring us our first computer(desktop) along with a CD filled up with games. Me and my cousin used to play with it all day. Although we used it only to play video games back in the day I was far more inspired my how it actually worked and after when I was 13 years old I began to open it up and tried to explored its components.I will also remeber the day it stoped working I was heart broken.

When I was 16 years old I began to work as a summer IT teacher for elementary students at a school called Ethio international School in Bahirdar. And it helped to develop my confidence and leadership skills. And it was also the first place that I wrote my first lines of code and I loved it, ever since I have always had a love for computer programming and everything about it.
